Product Design

Designing Aesthetic & Functional Products

Using Adobe InDesign and Illustrator

Designing aesthetically on products that showcase the brand, is integral in generating exposure as they are more likely to be worn, photographed and shared by users; thus, seen by their peers who could be part of our target audience as well.

My process for creating these designs include ideation, considering the theme and respective brand guidelines while aligning with product functionality through templates. I leverage my detail orientedness to ensure high quality production by making sure the design is set at a high resolution of 300 dpi and the delivery of products is done in a timely way.
