Spark Foundation Strategy

SPARK Foundation, a youth education and life-skill program, was looking to improve its website and search ranking

Impact: Improve the website’s UX (navigation and design consistency), provide more context on their programs and increase its ranking on organic google search results to ensure audiences’ information needs are quickly met

Key Contributions:

•  Identified key problems on the website

•  Analyzed customer and user pain points through UX testing on the website

•  Analyzed and selected different web builder platforms, based on SPARK Foundation’s needs and resources

•  Wrote, edited and embedded relevant keywords into current website content

•  Identified more ways: meta tags, Google My Business and backlinking to boost SEO

•  Provided explanations and practical examples to explain our strategy to a non-technical audience
